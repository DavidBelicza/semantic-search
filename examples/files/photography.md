# Taking better photos

## Light

Good light matters more than an expensive camera. Soft morning and evening light
is kind to almost any subject, while harsh midday sun casts hard shadows.

## Composition

Place your subject off center and give it room to breathe. Clean up the edges of
the frame so nothing distracting sneaks in.

## Keep it steady

A blurry photo is usually camera shake. Brace your elbows, breathe out, and press
the shutter gently.
