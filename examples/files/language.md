# Learning a new language

## Daily practice

A little every day beats a long session once a week. Short, regular exposure is
how vocabulary and grammar actually stick.

## Speaking early

Start speaking before you feel ready. Mistakes are how you learn, and waiting for
perfect grammar only slows you down.

## Immersion at home

Change your phone language, watch shows with subtitles, and label things around
the house. Surround yourself with the language without leaving home.
