# Starting a fitness habit

## Building the routine

Start small enough that it feels easy, so you actually show up. Two or three
short sessions a week beats one long session you dread. Put it on the calendar
and treat it like any other appointment.

## Warming up

A few minutes of light movement raises your heart rate and loosens joints before
harder work. Skipping it is the fastest way to a pulled muscle.

## Rest and recovery

Muscles grow between workouts, not during them. Sleep and a rest day let the body
repair, so more is not always better.
