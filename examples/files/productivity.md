# Staying focused at work

## Single tasking

Doing one thing at a time is faster than juggling several. Close the tabs you do
not need and silence notifications while you work on something that matters.

## Time blocking

Give each part of the day a job. A calendar full of blocks protects deep work
from the endless stream of small requests.

## Taking breaks

Short breaks keep attention fresh. Step away, look at something far off, and let
your mind reset before the next block.
